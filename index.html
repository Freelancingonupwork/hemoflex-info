Delete Account & Data

Hemoflex is a privacy-first, offline application. Your data stays where it belongs—on your device.

How to remove your data

  1. In-App Method Open the app → Go to Side Menu Settings → Select "Delete my profile"
  2. System Settings Settings → Apps → Hemoflex → Storage → "Clear storage" or "Clear data"
  3. Uninstall App Removing the app from your device automatically deletes all local data

Already uninstalled?

Since Hemoflex does not store data on any external servers, once the app is uninstalled, your data is permanently removed from your device. There is no server-side footprint to delete.
